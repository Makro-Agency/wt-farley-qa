QA ran in three passes: a storefront functional pass and data integrity pass (10 to 13 July), a deep configurator audit that compared every option group and price against the legacy Shift4Shop export (11 to 12 July), and a full automated re-verification on 22 July after the latest configurator work went live. 3. What passed

Configurator (31 live products)

  • Engine data valid on every product; no template errors on any page.
  • No required option group renders empty; no duplicate part-number positions; part codes present on every orderable choice.
  • Part number and price assemble correctly as options are picked; both stay blank until the shopper makes a first selection.
  • Preview panel behavior (new, 22 Jul): grey placeholder on load with product-specific text, nothing pre-selected, main image appears after the first pick, Color Coding selections swap the image on all 4 click regulators, and the layered hose preview is live on all 9 gas hoses.
  • Gas-type switcher swaps sibling products without a page reload; add to cart carries the full selection as line-item properties; incomplete builds are blocked with a nudge to the missing option.

Part-number lookup

  • Lookup API live and healthy; a legacy part number (OH-036) resolves and returns the right product suggestions.
  • Full part numbers prefill the whole configuration; partial part numbers prefill what matches and prompt for the rest.
  • Cross-product handoff works: entering a part number on the wrong product routes to the right one.

Storefront and data

  • Key pages all load clean: home, collections, search, cart, contact, about, FAQ, dealer application, testimonials, policies.
  • Legacy URL redirects return 301 (see fixed issue #4). No empty collections. Free-text search healthy.
  • Migration counts verified exactly: 17,054 orders, 11,274 customers, 92 collections, 760 active products.

4. Issues found and fixed

These were found by our QA passes, fixed by the team, and re-verified on the live store on 22 July.

IssueFoundStatusVerification
Air Hose configurator missing its three core option groups (hose length, inlet, outlet were in draft status behind the scenes), so a valid part number could not be built.12 JulFixedAll 6 groups live with full choice lists.
15-Foot Coiled Air Hose: a required option had zero visible choices (all four were flagged hidden), which could block ordering.12 JulFixedGroup renders with its choices.
MRI Conditional Vacuum Hose showed the outlet picker twice, risking an ambiguous part number.12 JulFixedSingle outlet group, positions unique.
Legacy site URLs had no redirects: old links and search results would have landed on 404s after launch.10 JulFixedSampled legacy paths now return 301 to the right pages. One older URL pattern still being extended (see open items).
Two internal test products were live on the storefront at a $999,999 placeholder price.12 JulFixedBoth return 404 on the storefront.
34 migrated product descriptions carried a legacy shipping notice with an out-of-date phone number, plus leftover Word markup from the old site.21 JulFixedNotice and markup removed across all 827 product descriptions.
Configurator pages showed the product photo and pre-selected options on load, before the shopper chose anything.22 JulFixedPlaceholder until first pick, nothing pre-selected, part number and price start blank. Layered hose products keep their existing behavior.
Gas-type tiles misaligned on manifold products: banner artwork was cropped and tiles sat at different heights.22 JulFixedTiles top-aligned; wide artwork shows uncropped; hose tiles unchanged.
Preview placeholder text read awkwardly on some products ("Select a Choose Length..."), caught by the same-day automated re-check.22 JulFixedCopy reads correctly on all 22 placeholder products.
Product card images downloaded far larger than displayed on desktop (full-screen renditions for card-size slots) on collection pages, search and homepage sliders.22 JulFixedBrowsers now pick card-size renditions: 50 to 70% less image transfer per card (sample: 52KB to 24KB or less).
Placeholder links in the footer (COA Finder, Catalog, Locations) went nowhere.10 JulFixedNo dead placeholder links render in the footer.
Part numbers typed into the regular search returned nothing (composite part numbers are not stock SKUs, so free-text search cannot find them).10 JulFixedPart-number-shaped queries now switch the header search to Lookup automatically and resolve there; normal words and phrases stay in regular search.
Sample test part numbers were embedded in the header lookup markup on every page.10 JulFixedStatic samples removed; the suggestion list is populated only from real lookup results.

6. Performance

Measured on 22 July over the public storefront, using compressed transfer sizes (what a real browser downloads).

MeasureResultReading
Homepage HTML (compressed)74KBHealthy. The page is markup-heavy but compresses well.
Product page HTML (compressed)49 to 54KBHealthy, including the configurator engine data.
Product card images on desktop50 to 70% smaller after fixCards previously downloaded full-screen renditions; browsers now pick card-size ones (sample image: 52KB before, 14 to 24KB after). Collection pages and homepage sliders benefit on every card.
Third-party scripts~110KB platform baselineShopify checkout wallets and analytics loader, standard on every Shopify store, plus the consent, reviews and email apps. Nothing anomalous.

All performance changes were delivery hints only: no feature, layout or visual behavior was touched, and the configurator, layered previews and galleries were re-verified after the change. One recommendation for WT Farley: a few homepage images are very large PNG source files (largest 1.14MB); modern browsers receive an optimized version automatically, but replacing the heaviest source files with compressed exports would benefit older browsers.
