How to QA your new store.

A page-by-page guide for your team: what to open, what to click, and what to confirm on each part of the site. Work through it at your own pace, on a computer and on a phone.

DateJuly 22, 2026
Storewt-farley.myshopify.com
Time neededAbout an hour, once
Before you start

Three things to know.

01 · The routine

Open, click, confirm

Each slide covers one part of the site: the page to open and a short list of checks. Do each check once on desktop, then repeat the marked ones on a phone.

02 · Recording results

Tick the shared sheet

We shared a Validation Matrix spreadsheet with the same checks as this deck. Mark Desktop pass and Mobile pass per row, with your name.

03 · If something looks off

Use the Report Issue tab

The orange tab on the right edge of the preview captures the page and your note in one click. We review submissions daily.

Page 1 of 9 · Homepage

Start at the front door.

wt-farley.myshopify.com
01Scroll the whole page. Every section should have final imagery and copy, nothing that looks like placeholder content.
02Click through the Shop by Category grid. Each of the 18 cards should open the matching category.
03Try the product sliders: arrows move them, and each card opens the right product.
04Scroll down and back up. The header should stay with you the whole way.
Page 2 of 9 · Menu and navigation

Walk the menu like a customer would.

Any page · top menu
01Open the main menu and hover each top item. The flyout should show the same structure you had on the old site.
02Spot-check 5 to 10 menu links across different sections. Each should land on the right category with products in it.
03Check the footer: social links, policy pages, and that every link goes somewhere real.
04Type a nonsense address like /pages/xyz. You should get the styled page-not-found screen with a way back in.
Page 3 of 9 · Search and part-number lookup

Search like a customer, then like a technician.

Header search bar
01Type a word like "hose" or "regulator". Matching categories and products should appear as you type.
02Press Enter. The full results page should show the grid with working filters.
03Switch to Lookup and enter a real part number from one of your old invoices. It should land on the right product with the options already selected.
04Try a partial part number. It should fill what it can and ask you to complete the rest.
05Try a made-up part number. You should get a clear message with your phone number for help.
Good test data Pull 3 to 5 part numbers from real past orders, ideally different product families: a hose, a regulator, a pigtail.
Page 4 of 9 · Category pages

Browse a category end to end.

/collections/hose-assemblies-for-medical-gas · or any category
01Check the banner, category name and product count look right.
02Apply a filter, then a second one. The list should narrow correctly; clearing filters should bring everything back.
03Scroll to the bottom. More products should load automatically as you go.
04Scan the cards: right photo, right title, right price. Flag any product that looks wrong to you as the catalog owner.
05Repeat on one or two more categories you know well.
Page 5 of 9 · Product pages

Check a few products you know by heart.

Any regular (non-configurator) product
01Photos: main image, thumbnails, zoom. Description reads clean, no leftover formatting from the old site.
02Pick each size or option in the dropdown. Price and part number should follow your selection.
03Open the Documents and Specifications sections; download a document.
04Scroll down: the add-to-cart bar should stay within reach.
05Add to cart and confirm the right variant and price appear in the cart.
Good test data Pick your 5 best sellers plus 2 products with many variants. You know what these should say better than anyone.
Page 6 of 9 · Configurator: gas hoses

Build a hose, start to finish.

/products/oxygen-hose
01On arrival: options wait behind the gas choice, and part number and price show a dash.
02Pick a gas type. The options appear. Switch gas and confirm the page follows without reloading.
03Choose length, inlet and outlet. Watch the part number assemble and the price climb, and check both against your catalog for that exact build.
04Watch the preview: the chosen fittings should appear on the ends of the hose.
05Try adding to cart with an option missing. It should stop you and point at what is missing.
06Complete the build and add to cart. The cart line should list the part number and every choice you made.
Repeat on One or two more hoses (vacuum, nitrogen) and one MRI hose, using builds you sell often.
Page 7 of 9 · Configurator: regulators and more

Then build a regulator.

/products/oxygen-standard-body-click-regulator · /products/15-foot-coiled-air-hose
01On arrival the preview panel shows a prompt instead of a photo, and nothing is chosen for you.
02Pick the first option. The product photo appears and the part number starts.
03On the click regulator, open Color Coding and click through the colors. The photo should change to the color you picked.
04Hit Reset Configurator. Everything returns to the start.
05Finish a build and confirm part number and price against your catalog.
Repeat on The manifolds, the vacuum regulators, the pigtails and the flowmeter system, with a build you know for each.
Page 8 of 9 · Cart and checkout

Take an order to the doorstep of checkout.

Cart icon · top right
01With a few items in the cart, open it: photos, names, prices and the subtotal should be right.
02Change a quantity and remove an item. Totals should update instantly.
03Check a configured product's cart line: part number and all chosen options listed.
04Continue to checkout. Items and totals carry over, and shipping options appear for your address.
Page 9 of 9 · Content pages and forms

Read your own pages.

/pages/about-us · /pages/faq · /pages/contact · /pages/become-a-wt-farley-dealer · /pages/customer-testimonials
01About Us and FAQ: read the copy as the owner. Wording, claims and contact details are yours to sign off.
02Send a test message through the contact form and a test dealer application, and confirm both arrive on your side.
03Testimonials: 20 quotes, names and companies spelled right.
04Policy pages: shipping, returns, terms all say what you expect.
One more pass · then you are done

Now do the highlights on a phone.

01On a phone, repeat: one configurator build, one search, one category browse, one add to cart. Everything should be usable with one thumb.
02Mark each row in the shared Validation Matrix as you go: Desktop pass, Mobile pass, your name.
03Anything that looks off, send it through the Report Issue tab on the right edge of the page.
04When the sheet is green, we lock the launch checklist together.

Questions while testing? Message us any time. We would rather hear about it twice than not at all.
