Part one

The configurator.

The heart of the store: 31 products where a shopper builds a hose, regulator, manifold or stand option by option, and the part number, price and preview build with them. We will click through three products together.

Walkthrough 1 · Gas hose with layered preview

Build an oxygen hose, watch the preview assemble itself.

01Open /products/oxygen-hose. Options sit behind the gas choice; part number and price show a dash.
02Pick a gas type. The options reveal, and switching gas later swaps the whole product without a page reload.
03Pick a hose length, inlet and outlet. The part number assembles piece by piece and the price climbs with each add-on.
04Watch the image panel: the hose renders in layers, and the chosen fittings appear on the hose ends.
05Add to cart. Open the cart: every selection is listed on the line item, with the finished part number.
06Try adding with a required option missing: the page blocks it and scrolls you to the missing choice.
What to validate The part number matches what your team would quote for the same build, and the price matches your list plus add-ons.
Walkthrough 2 · New this week

Products no longer show a finished photo before you choose.

Before

Photo and pre-picked options on load

  • The product photo showed immediately, even for a build-to-order product
  • Some options arrived pre-selected
  • Part number and price displayed before any choice was made
Now, live on the store

Nothing until the shopper chooses

  • Grey panel with product-specific text, e.g. "Select a Flow Rate to Preview Your Regulator"
  • No option pre-selected; part number and price start blank
  • First pick reveals the product photo; Reset returns to the grey panel
Try it Open /products/15-foot-coiled-air-hose. Confirm the grey panel, pick an inlet, watch the photo and part number appear, then hit Reset Configurator.
Walkthrough 3 · Click regulators

Color Coding changes the picture to the color you picked.

01Open /products/oxygen-standard-body-click-regulator. Grey panel reads "Select a Flow Rate to Preview Your Regulator".
02Pick a flow rate. The regulator photo appears and the part number starts building.
03Open Color Coding and pick Green, then Blue, then Red. The photo swaps to the matching color each time.
04Finish the remaining options and confirm the finished part number matches your catalog for that build.
Same behavior All four click regulators carry the color swap. Wall outlet manifolds show their gas artwork uncropped on the gas tiles, fixed this morning after your note.
Part two · Part-number lookup

A customer with a part number gets a ready-to-buy page.

Path A · Header

Switch the header search to Lookup and enter a part number from an old invoice.

Path B · Product page

Use the "Have a part number?" bar at the top of any configurator page.

↓

Both land on the right product with the configuration prefilled. A full part number fills every option; a partial one fills what it can and asks the shopper to complete the rest.

↓

Entering a part number on the wrong product page routes to the right product automatically.

Honest note Legacy composite part numbers resolve through suggestions rather than an exact match, because they never existed as single SKUs. Typing a part number into the regular search (not Lookup) returns nothing; we recommend routing those queries to Lookup automatically and we cover it in training.
